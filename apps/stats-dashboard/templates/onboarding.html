iPad Setup Tips

Before game day, configure your iPad for the best experience. More detail is available in the Operator app under Settings → iPad Tips.

📲
Add to Home Screen
In Safari: tap the Share button → Add to Home Screen. Launches full-screen, no browser chrome.
💡
Auto-Lock: Never
Settings → Display & Brightness → Auto-Lock → Never
↩️
Shake to Undo: Off
Settings → Accessibility → Touch → Shake to Undo → Off
🔕
Do Not Disturb: On
Prevents notifications and interruptions mid-game. Enable from Control Center or Settings → Focus.
🔄
Rotation Lock: On
Lock orientation to avoid accidental rotations. Swipe down from top-right corner → tap the Rotation Lock icon.
📶
WiFi + Bluetooth: On
WiFi required for live stat sync. Bluetooth needed if using peripherals or a keyboard.
