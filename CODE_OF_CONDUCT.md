# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes:

- Being respectful and constructive in communication
- Accepting feedback with professionalism
- Focusing discussion on the work and outcomes

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks, trolling, or disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project spaces, issue and PR discussions, and community communications related to this repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, lock threads, or block contributors for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately through the security and support channels listed in `SECURITY.md` and `SUPPORT.md`.
